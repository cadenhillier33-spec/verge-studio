FAQ

Questions we
always get.

Do I own the website once it’s built?+
Yes, completely. Once the project is complete, you own every line of code, every image, every word. It’s yours. We don’t hold anything hostage or charge ongoing fees to keep it live.
What exactly is included in $1,500?+
Everything. Custom design, full development, mobile optimization, SEO setup, contact forms, domain and hosting setup, unlimited revisions, and 30 days of post-launch support. No add-ons, no hidden fees.
What if I need changes after launch?+
Changes within the first 30 days are included. After that, we offer simple hourly support or small retainer packages — but many clients handle minor updates themselves since we build on platforms that are easy to edit.
Do I need to provide content or photos?+
We prefer to use your real content — photos of your business, your story, your words. But if you don’t have professional photos, we can use high-quality stock imagery and we’ll write all the copy for you. You just answer our questions and we handle the rest.
How does the 14-day timeline actually work?+
Day 1 is our discovery call. Days 2–3 we plan the structure. Days 4–8 we design and build. Days 9–11 you review and we revise. Days 12–13 we test everything. Day 14 we launch. The timeline holds as long as we receive your feedback within 24 hours during the review stage.
Will my site show up on Google?+
We set up all the technical SEO foundations — proper page titles, meta descriptions, site speed, mobile optimization, and Google Search Console. This gives you a strong base. Ranking takes time and depends on your market, but we make sure everything that can be done on launch day is done.
What if I’m not happy with the result?+
We keep working until you are. Unlimited revisions means exactly that. In the rare case we genuinely can’t get it right, we’ll refund you completely. We’ve never had to issue a refund — but the guarantee is real.
Do you work with businesses outside Connecticut?+
Yes. Everything we do is remote-friendly. We’ve built sites for businesses across the US. Our discovery calls and review sessions happen over video. Location doesn’t affect anything.
